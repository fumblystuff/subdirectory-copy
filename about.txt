Multiple Source Folder Copy (msfcopy) is a utility that allows you to manage copying multiple subdirectories from one or more source directories. With the utility, you to copy some, but not all of the subfolders from specific directory to the root of a removable drive (flash disk).

Designed specifically to help copy a subset of artist folders from a Music directory (like an iTunes Media folder, for example) to a flash drive so you can play the music in your car.
